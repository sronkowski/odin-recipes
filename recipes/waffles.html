Waffles

Description

Who doesn't love a good waffle? This recipe will make a wonderful waffle perfect for your weekend mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-rising flour (White Lily is best)
  • 4 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients into a bowl and stir to combine
  2. Make a well in flour mixture, and add in wet ingredients
  3. Stir only enough to combine - lumps are okay!
  4. Pour enough batter to fill your waffle-maker
  5. Cook according to waffle-maker instructions
  6. Eat and enjoy!
